We provide solutions to process payments and manage funds.

We have 8 different solutions for managing online bill pay, automatic payments, refunds, and a variety of other payment options.

Services

eBill & ePay

Save money, increase cash flow, and improve service to students by presenting bills electronically and allowing online payments in a PCI Level 1 compliant environment.

Actively Mangaged Payment Plans

Increase affordability, reduce administrative burdens, and improve access by allowing students to budget tuition and fee payments according to a schedule defined by your institution.

Student Choice Refunds

Offer an alternative refund method that limits fee exposure to students, promotes choice, and protects your institution from increased scrutiny surrounding refunds disbursement.

eStore

Sell goods, process event registrations, and solicit donations – all within a single, self-service web store with shopping cart functionality, inventory management, and more.

Cashiering

Integrate in-person payments into your online payment gateway, streamline the process at the cashier’s window, and automate the manual tasks associated with departmental deposits.

Hosted Payments

Allow departments across campus to securely accept credit/debit card or ACH payments without capturing or storing card information, keeping your institution secure and compliant.

Sponsored Payments

Offer an electronic payment gateway to corporations or organizations that wish to sponsor students by paying part or all of their tuition and fees.

Refunds

Offer an electronic payment gateway to corporations or organizations that wish to sponsor students by paying part or all of their tuition and fees.
